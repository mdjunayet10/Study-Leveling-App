# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026

Study Leveling is designed to help students manage study tasks, track progress, earn rewards, and use leaderboard or multiplayer features.

Study Leveling is prepared for Web, Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, and Windows.

This Privacy Policy explains the basic privacy approach for the public version of the app.

---

## Information the App May Use

Depending on how a user uses the app, Study Leveling may use:

- Account name or username
- Email address for login
- Google Sign-In information if the user chooses Google login
- Study progress
- XP, coins, and level data
- Leaderboard score
- Multiplayer room participation data
- Profile picture if the user adds one

---

## Firebase Services

Study Leveling may use Firebase services such as:

- Firebase Authentication
- Firebase Realtime Database
- Firebase Hosting

These services help provide login, leaderboard, multiplayer rooms, and hosting.

---

## Guest Use

Users may be able to use parts of the app without logging in.

Guest or local progress may be stored locally on the user's device or browser. This local progress may be lost if the user clears browser/app data or changes devices.

---

## Leaderboard and Multiplayer

If users use leaderboard or multiplayer features, some public or shared study progress information may be visible to other users, such as:

- Username
- Rank
- Level
- XP
- Completed tasks count
- Room participation progress

Users should avoid using private personal information as their username.

---

## Data Security

The app is designed to use Firebase security features and database rules to protect data. However, users should not share sensitive personal information inside usernames, task names, room names, or profile fields.

---

## Children's Privacy

Study Leveling is made for students. Young users should use the app with permission or guidance from a parent, guardian, or teacher where required.
