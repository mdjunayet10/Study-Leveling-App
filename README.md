# Study Leveling App

**Study Leveling** is a gamified study productivity app that turns studying into a game.

Instead of only making a normal to-do list, Study Leveling lets students complete study tasks, earn XP, collect coins, level up, unlock rewards, track progress, and compete with others through leaderboards and multiplayer study rooms.

The goal of the app is simple:

> Make studying feel more motivating, rewarding, and game-like.

This public repository is used for the official app page, release notes, license, screenshots, and public downloads.

The full application source code is private.

## What Makes Study Leveling Different?

Study Leveling is built around a **game-style learning system**.

Students do not just write down tasks. They play through their study journey by:

- Completing study missions
- Earning XP
- Collecting coins
- Leveling up
- Unlocking rewards
- Tracking progress
- Competing on the leaderboard
- Studying together with friends

The app is designed to make students feel like every completed task is progress in a game.

---

## Core Game System

### Study Tasks as Missions

Students can create study tasks and treat them like missions.

Examples:

- Finish math homework
- Read one chapter
- Memorize vocabulary
- Complete a practice test
- Review class notes
- Study for 30 minutes

When a task is completed, the student earns rewards and the task is counted in progress/history.

---

### XP System

Every completed task gives XP.

XP helps the student grow their level and see long-term improvement.

The XP system is designed to make studying feel rewarding, even when the task is small.

---

### Leveling System

As students earn XP, they level up.

The level system gives students a sense of progress and achievement, similar to games where players grow stronger over time.

Instead of leveling up by fighting enemies, students level up by studying.

---

### Coins System

Students also earn coins by completing tasks.

Coins can be used inside the reward shop.

This gives students an extra reason to stay consistent and complete their study goals.

---

### Rewards Shop

The rewards shop is designed to motivate students in a halal-friendly and student-friendly way.

Example rewards can include:

- Book or course voucher
- Stationery reward
- Study break reward
- Halal food or cafe treat
- Learning resource
- Charity or sadaqah reward

The reward system is meant to encourage healthy motivation, not distraction.

---

## Progress Tracking

Study Leveling helps students see how much they have done.

Students can track:

- Completed tasks
- XP
- Coins
- Level
- Study progress
- Long-term improvement

This helps students stay aware of their learning habits and feel proud of their consistency.

---

## Global Leaderboard

Study Leveling includes a global ranking system powered by Firebase.

The leaderboard allows students to compare progress and stay motivated.

Leaderboard features include:

- Global ranking list
- Top 3 ranking badges
- Current user highlight
- XP and level comparison
- Completed task progress

The leaderboard is designed to encourage friendly competition, not pressure.

---

## Multiplayer Study Mode

Study Leveling includes multiplayer study features so students can study together.

There are two multiplayer modes:

---

### Home Multiplayer

Home Multiplayer is for students studying together in the same place or on the same device.

It is useful for:

- Friends studying together
- Family study sessions
- Classroom challenges
- Group motivation

---

### Away Multiplayer

Away Multiplayer is for students studying from different devices.

Students can create or join online study rooms using room codes.

Away Multiplayer includes:

- Create online study rooms
- Join rooms using a room code
- Cross-device room sync
- Room leaderboard
- Room-only tasks
- Participant progress tracking
- Live XP, coin, and task updates

This makes it possible for friends to study together even when they are not in the same location.

---

## Account System

Study Leveling supports account features while still allowing users to use the app freely.

Account features include:

- Login
- Sign up
- Google Sign-In
- Email/password login
- Password reset
- Profile picture support
- User progress sync

Users can still use the app without logging in, but some online features may work better with an account.
